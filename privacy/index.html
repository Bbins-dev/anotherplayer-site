Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-13

This Privacy Policy explains how Binbox Games ("we", "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use AnotherPlayer ("the Software") and visit anotherplayer.com.

1. Data we collect

  • Email address — collected by Paddle (our payment processor) when you purchase a Commercial license.
  • License key — generated by us and stored in our database to validate your license.
  • Install UUID — a randomly generated identifier (GUID v4) created on first install of the Software, stored locally in your settings file. This is not a hardware fingerprint; it is per-installation and resets on reinstall.
  • Crash log — limited diagnostic information sent only when the Software crashes and you opt to send it.

We do not collect: name, address, phone number, IP geolocation, browsing history, contact list, microphone/camera input, files on your device.

2. How we use it

  • License issuance and validation
  • Email delivery (license key, important account notices)
  • Payment record-keeping (legal/tax obligation)
  • Crash diagnosis and software improvement

3. Sharing (processors)

We share data only with the following processors, strictly to provide the service:

Processor Role Data shared Location
Paddle Payment / Merchant of Record Email, payment details UK
Cloudflare Hosting, Workers, D1 database All operational data US
Resend Transactional email delivery Email address US

We do not sell or share personal information for advertising or third-party marketing.

4. Retention

Data Retention period
License key + Install UUID Lifetime of the license
Payment records 5 years (Korean tax law)
Crash logs 90 days

5. International transfer

Your personal information may be transferred from the Republic of Korea to the United States (Cloudflare, Resend) and the United Kingdom (Paddle). Required disclosures per Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) Article 28-8:

  • Legal basis: Performance of contract (license delivery) and legitimate interest (service operation).
  • Data transferred: Email, license key, Install UUID, payment metadata.
  • Recipient countries: United States (Cloudflare, Resend), United Kingdom (Paddle).
  • Transfer date and method: Real-time, via HTTPS API calls during normal operation.
  • Recipient name and contact: Cloudflare, Inc. (US) — privacy@cloudflare.com; Resend, Inc. (US) — privacy@resend.com; Paddle.com Market Ltd. (UK) — privacy@paddle.com.
  • Purpose of use by recipient: Payment processing, hosting, email delivery.
  • Retention by recipient: Per each processor's own privacy policy.
  • How to refuse: You may refuse by not purchasing or using the Software; required for service provision.

You have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — to know what data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — to request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18) — to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — to receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — to object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making (Art. 22) — we do not make automated decisions with legal effect.
  • Right to be informed (Art. 13-14) — fulfilled by this Privacy Policy.

To exercise these rights, contact contact@binboxgames.com.
