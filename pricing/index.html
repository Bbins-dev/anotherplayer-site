FAQ

Is AnotherPlayer really free?

Yes. v1 is free for every user and every use case — personal, freelance, studio, commercial. No license key, no registration, no time limit, no nag screen.

Will it stay free?

Yes. v1 stays free for everyone, permanently.

How can I support development?

You can support development with an optional donation — see the tiers above. Any amount is welcome and entirely optional. Sharing AnotherPlayer with other audio professionals and reporting issues to contact@binboxgames.com helps just as much.

Are there any restrictions on use?

You may not redistribute, resell, sublicense, or reverse-engineer the Software. See Terms of Service for the full license grant. Other than that, use it however you want.

Why is it free?

AnotherPlayer is built for the sample-browsing workflow that audio professionals deal with every day. Making v1 free for everyone gets it into more hands faster — which makes the tool better through real-world feedback.
